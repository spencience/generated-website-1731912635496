Simple Platformer Game

Gameplay

Navigate through increasingly challenging levels, collecting power-ups and avoiding obstacles to reach the goal.

Levels

Progress through 10 unique levels, each with its own set of challenges and obstacles.

  • Level 1: Forest
  • Level 2: Desert
  • Level 3: Mountain
  • ...

Power-ups

Collect power-ups to gain an advantage, such as increased speed or invincibility.

  • Speed Boost
  • Invincibility
  • Shield
